The dry-run pipeline shape worked cleanly for a tiny one-shot sanity pass: the screenplay, velocity, storyboard, music, and shot generation calls all have cheap deterministic surfaces, and the local renderer is enough to produce a real MP4 without paid spend. What surprised me was that the commercial workflow definition still expects edit-stage artifacts like `transitions.json`, `cuts.edl`, `captions.json`, plus asset directories even for a deliberately minimal single-shot run, so I filled those with lightweight local outputs/placeholders after exercising the dry-run calls. Next time I would add a dedicated `commercial-mini` pipeline profile whose required artifacts match the smallest possible render path.
