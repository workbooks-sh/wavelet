# Strategy — KitchenAid Artisan stand mixer · 12s · 9:16 Reels

## Brand resolution

User asked for a "Whirlpool ad for their iconic stand mixer." The iconic
stand mixer ships under Whirlpool's subsidiary brand **KitchenAid**
(acquired 1986). All identity assets, palette, type, and CTA copy
resolve to KitchenAid — not the Whirlpool parent mark.

Source: `adalign brief kitchenaid.com` (this workdir, 2026-05-23).

## Brand identity (from adalign)

- Brand name: **KitchenAid**
- Logo URL: `https://media.brand.dev/26ac1fee-3ac5-48eb-aaf1-db6a83a0c05c.png`
- Palette: `#c61430` (signature red), `#2c2c2c` (charcoal), `#d48494` (rose)
- Slogan: "Bringing culinary inspiration to life since 1919."
- Type: Avenir (display + body)
- Hero product: Artisan Series Tilt-Head Stand Mixer — instantly
  recognizable silhouette

## CTA mode

`CTA mode: lifestyle` — reason: premium brief, 12s reels duration,
KitchenAid's iconic mixer is recognized on sight; a "shop now"
direct-response card would cheapen the spot. Last 2.0s is an HTML
close (no Veo) with the real KitchenAid wordmark, the brand's
since-1919 line, and a small mixer silhouette mark. No button, no URL.

## Cinematography lock

```
50mm full-frame, medium-close framing, warm morning window light from
camera-left at 3200K, deep cream-flour and brick-red palette, soft
shadow contrast, gentle film grain, A24 domestic-warm grade, shallow
depth of field, locked 9:16 portrait
```

Every Veo prompt ends with the above clause character-for-character.
The lock holds across all four shots — no register breaks.

## Shot count and pacing

5 cuts across 12s, hard-cut throughout. No crossfades — every cut
reads as decisive editorial intent. 4 Veo hero clips trimmed to the
useful window + 1 HTML close card.
