# Strategy — Whirlpool stand mixer (12s, 9:16, Meta Reels)

## Concept

"The heart of home." A wordless three-shot meditation on the iconic
cream-white stand mixer at work in a warm morning kitchen. The mixer
is the protagonist; no people, no dialogue, just light, surface, and
motion. Lifestyle-mode close — wordmark sign-off, no hard CTA button.

## Shot list (3 × 4s = 12s)

1. **Hero establish** — wide low-angle of the cream tilt-head mixer
   on a thick marble counter, warm morning window light raking across
   from camera-left, dust motes drifting, the head lifting open in
   slow ceremony.
2. **Macro process** — extreme close on the spiral dough hook folding
   pale enriched dough inside the polished stainless bowl, butter
   streaks catching the light, slow rotation.
3. **Reveal + lockup** — slow pull-back: the mixer at rest beside a
   linen-draped boule of risen dough on a wooden board, steam from a
   coffee cup at the edge of frame; brand sign-off fades in on the
   last beat.

## Cinematography lock

50mm full-frame, medium-close framings, golden-hour window light from
camera-left at 3200K, deep cream-and-walnut palette with brushed-
chrome highlights, gentle film grain, A24 domestic-warm grade, locked
9:16 portrait, no whip pans

(Every Veo shot prompt appends this clause verbatim.)

## CTA mode

CTA mode: **lifestyle** — reason: premium heritage-appliance category
favors quiet brand sign-off over direct-response. Spot ends with
"Whirlpool" wordmark + "At the heart of home" tagline + small "Built
to last a lifetime" line. No button, no URL, no QR.

## Typography variation per scene

- Scene 1 (overlay during shot 1): editorial silence — no type at all,
  let the mixer breathe.
- Scene 2 (overlay during shot 2): tiny mono corner-tag — "EST. 1965 ·
  THE ARTISAN SILHOUETTE" upper-left, JetBrains Mono 13px, 0.3em
  tracking. Observational, restrained.
- Scene 3 (overlay during shot 3 + sign-off): wordmark fades in
  center-low in Bodoni Moda 700, "At the heart of home" italic kicker
  beneath, "Built to last a lifetime" all-caps Inter 200 micro line at
  bottom inside title-safe.
