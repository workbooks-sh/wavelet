## Production approach

Budget cap was $5; available backends in this run = OpenRouter only (no Fal,
no Pexels, no ElevenLabs). Decision: zero-paid-asset spot, pure HTML/CSS/SVG
on Blitz/Vello. The mixer is rendered as an inline SVG (tilt-head silhouette,
chrome bowl, animated whisk) so we get product fidelity from primitives
without fabricating a photographic claim. Heritage / type-led editorial flow
carries the premium feel.
