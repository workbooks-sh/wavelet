KitchenAid (acquired 1986); the iconic stand mixer is the
**KitchenAid Artisan**. All brand surface comes from KitchenAid,
not Whirlpool's appliance line.

## CTA mode: direct_response

Reason: KitchenAid Meta ad sample (via Home Depot's KitchenAid push) ends
in "Shop now" + appliance-retail link. KitchenAid's own retail flow
("Shop the Artisan", "Free shipping", "Build your kitchen") is
direct-response. Final 2 s holds a CSS-typeset CTA card with real
wordmark, brand-red button, and `kitchenaid.com`.
